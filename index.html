Our Services

Island-Wide Speed

Documents and parcels zip across Sri Lanka within 24 hours, guaranteeing prompt and reliable delivery to any destination. Send and receive documents & parcels island-wide with our affordable options, whether for business or personal needs. Track your delivery every step of the way with our user-friendly system and receive Proof of Delivery confirmation for ultimate peace of mind.

Documents & Packages (Parcel Delivery)

Enjoy the convenience of doorstep pickup and delivery, at no extra cost, for any document or parcel across Sri Lanka. Whether it's a commercial shipment or a household item, we tailor cost-effective solutions to meet your specific needs. We deliver medical supplies, specimens, and samples with utmost care and precision, adhering to established healthcare standards.

Medical Delivery

Pharmacies, hospitals, clinics, and labs rely on our expertise for timely and secure medical courier services. Our trained and bonded couriers utilize specialized equipment like ice packs and Versapak containers to ensure optimal conditions for your medical deliveries. We understand the sensitivity of medical deliveries. Our efficient processes prioritize on-time arrival, security, and complete confidentiality.

Urban Urgency

Urgent documents and parcels get same-day pickup and delivery in key Sri Lankan cities and towns, meeting your critical needs with speed. We understand the importance of time-sensitive deliveries. Our dedicated team ensures your packages reach their destination promptly and securely.
